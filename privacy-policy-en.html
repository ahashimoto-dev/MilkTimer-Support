Privacy Policy

MilkTimer

Last Updated: December 17, 2025

1. Introduction

The developer of MilkTimer (hereinafter referred to as "the App") respects and protects the privacy of users. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled in the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 No Personal Information Collection

The App does not collect any personal information. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or addresses.

2.2 Usage Data

The App stores the following data locally on your device. This data is never transmitted to external servers:

  • Feeding time settings
  • Interval time settings
  • Left/Right display preference
  • Starting side preference
  • Notification sound preference

3. Purpose of Data Use

Data stored on your device is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To remember and restore your previous settings
  • To ensure proper app functionality

4. Data Sharing

The App does not share any user data with third parties. All data remains stored exclusively on your device and is never transmitted externally.

5. Analytics and Advertising

The App does not use any of the following services:

  • Analytics tools such as Google Analytics
  • Advertising networks
  • Crash reporting tools
  • Any other third-party services

6. Data Storage and Deletion

All data stored by the App is kept on your device. Uninstalling the App will delete all stored data.

7. Security

The App uses iOS standard security features for data storage. However, device security (such as passcode settings) is the user's responsibility.

8. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. As stated above, the App does not collect any personal information at all.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as necessary. Any changes will be posted on this page. For significant changes, we may also notify users within the App.
